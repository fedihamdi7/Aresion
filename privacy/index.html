Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Aresion ("the app") is a personal fitness tracking app developed by Fedi Hamdi. Your personal fitness data stays on your device. We do not run our own servers and we do not collect or sell your personal data. The app uses your device location only to fetch live weather data from a public API — your coordinates are never stored or shared. The app also uses Firebase Analytics for anonymous usage statistics.

2. Data You Enter

The following data is saved locally on your device only and is never transmitted to the developer or any third party:

  • Fitness and workout data (exercises, sets, reps, weights, personal records)
  • Meal entries and nutritional information
  • Body weight, goal weight, and body measurements
  • Progress photos
  • Water intake and custom progress trackers
  • Profile information (name, age, height, country, fitness goals)
  • App preferences and settings

3. Camera and Photo Access

Camera and photo library access is used solely to take progress photos and attach photos to tracker entries. Photos are stored locally and never uploaded to any server.

4. Automatic Backup

Android Auto Backup is disabled. Your data is not automatically backed up to Google or any cloud service. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data unless you have manually created a backup using the in-app backup feature.

5. Notifications

The app sends local reminders (workout, meal, water, and custom tracker reminders). These are generated on your device and do not involve external servers.

6. Location Access (Weather Feature)

Your approximate GPS coordinates are sent to the Open-Meteo weather API (open-meteo.com) to retrieve current weather data. This is a public, privacy-respecting API with no account or API key required. Your location is never stored in the app's database, never shared with advertisers, and never used for any purpose other than fetching weather. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in device Settings.

7. Firebase Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (screens opened, features used, crash reports, device model — all anonymized). Your personal fitness data is never collected. Firebase data is processed by Google under their privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

8. In-App Purchases

The optional Pro subscription is processed entirely through Google Play. Payment information is handled by Google — we never see or store your payment details.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal fitness data with any third party or advertiser. The only third-party services are Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage data) and Open-Meteo (location for weather only). There are no advertising SDKs in this app.

10. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, you are always in full control:

  • Delete all data — use Settings → Clear All Data within the app, or uninstall the app
  • Export data — use Settings → Save a Backup at any time

To request deletion of any analytics data collected by Firebase, contact us at aresion02@gmail.com.
